## Idea and feedback credits
The v2 research agenda was shaped directly by the community discussion on the
original "poverty trap" video. Many of the model dimensions (a middle-class
continuum, generational transmission, effort efficiency, social networks,
opportunity as a power law, country/policy regimes) came from thoughtful viewer
comments. See `docs/README.md` section 1 for the analysis that turned that
feedback into the roadmap.

## How to get listed here
Open a pull request that lands a meaningful change (code, docs, literature
notes, or calibration data) and add yourself to this file in the same PR. See
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
